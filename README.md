# Waxroom 🎵

Explore, collect, and share your music library in an interactive 3D vinyl globe — right in your browser.

**→ [waxroom.studio](https://waxroom.studio)**

## Features

- **3D Vinyl Globe** — Your albums as rotating vinyl discs in a golden-angle spiral. Drag to rotate, pinch or scroll to warp the sphere's depth and size.
- **Full CRUD** — Add records manually or search the iTunes database. Edit details, set ratings, manage tracklists.
- **Genre Filtering** — Instantly filter by genre. The sphere animates to show only matching albums.
- **Last.fm Sync** — Connect your Last.fm profile to import your top albums or auto-collect scrobbles in real time.
- **Crate Inbox** — Albums captured while listening land in your crate. Add them to your room or discard — all from the globe.
- **Multi-Room Support** — Create and switch between multiple rooms, each with its own collection.
- **Community Feed** — Publish your room or explore other collectors' rooms with live playback sync.
- **Cloud Sync** — Sign in with Google or email to back up your rooms and crate across devices.
- **PWA** — Installable on any device. Works offline with cached assets.
- **No account required** — Start collecting immediately. Link a profile later to sync.
